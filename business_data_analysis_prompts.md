# Business & Data Analysis Prompts

## Prompt: Sales Data Analyzer

**Goal:**  
Analyze sales data and generate clear business insights.

**Prompt:**  
You are a business data analyst.  
Analyze the given sales data and identify:
- Key trends
- Performance changes over time
- Potential business risks
- Actionable recommendations for management  

Present the results in clear bullet points.

**Why this works:**  
- Clear role definition  
- Structured output expectations  
- Business-oriented thinking
